## Theme Comparison

### Flipbook Theme
**Aesthetic:** Hand-drawn notebook doodles during class.  
**Background:** Warm cream paper (#f5f1e8) with horizontal ruled lines.  
**Ink:** Blue ballpoint pen (#1a4a7a).  
**Details:** Red margin line, blue spiral binding holes, wobbly hand-drawn lines.  
**Mood:** Playful, nostalgic, casual creativity.

### Noir Theme
**Aesthetic:** Cinematic film noir with dramatic lighting.  
**Background:** Deep black (#121216) with subtle vignetting.  
**Ink:** Off-white (#e8e4e0) with glowing highlights.  
**Details:** Rim lighting, film grain texture, high contrast, spotlights.  
**Mood:** Intense, dramatic, high-stakes action.
